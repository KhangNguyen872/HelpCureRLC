About Us

Here at HelpCure we invest 100% of our funds, donations, and times into cancer research and helping people who have been less fortunate with cancer.

We accept anybody in need and do not discriminate on race, gender, or status. We make sure everybody feels welcomed and that they are in a healthy enviroment. We also make sure that at the end of the day not a single person will pay a bill.
